Pancakes

Description

A pancake recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 250 g flour
  • 600 ml milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Stir the eggs, milk, sugar, salt and flour to a smooth dough
  2. Heat the pan and put some oil in it
  3. Scoop the batter onto the pan
  4. Brown on both sides
  5. Enjoy!
